Typing Game

It is a Java game that measures typing speed and accuracy. Designed for multiple players, it divides the keyboard into halves to compete simultaneously. Players type random words in rounds where errors affect the score. After eliminations, the best players advance and the top three places are announced at the end. It's a fun and competitive experience to improve typing skills.
